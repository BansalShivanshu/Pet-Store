Garuda

Breed Eagle

Garuda is a bird creature from Hindu Mythology that has a mix of eagle and human features. He is the vehicle of God Vishnu. It represents birth and heaven and is the enemy of all snakes. Garuda traditionally has the torso and arms of a man and the wings, head, beak and talons of an eagle or vulture. His body is gold in colour, his wings are red and his face is white. Garuda is also known as the 'king of the birds', as 'he who has beautiful feathers', as 'golden bodied' and 'the devourer'.
